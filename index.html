USA'S Economy 2011-2021

This is a visulaisation to show some visualizations about the USA's Economy since 2011 until 2021. I will show some stats, for ex. GDP per state, which is a measure for the size of an economy per state. I will also show the average Income of person in each state of the USA.

The United States Economy is always considered one of the strongest economies in the world. But we all know that the United States is made of 50 states, which is alot. Not all the states are equally rich or poor, so that's what I will try to show in this'visualization. In this visualization, I'm trying to show the strong and the weak states by many econmical factors.

USA GDA BY STATE

This map shows the GDP in each state of the USA. It clearly shows that california is the state with the highest GDP. It also shows that Texas is the second state with GDP. Overall, the states does not share an equal GDP. Some states are doing better than the other, and this is due to many reasons.

USA AVERAGE GDA

This graph shows the average GDA of all states of the USA. It clearly shows that the GDA is increasing for the last 10 years, except 2020, where is was the start of the coronavirus pandemic. But it increased again in 2021.

AVERAGE INCOME PER PERSON IN EACH STATE

This bar chart shows the average income of person in each state. It is obvious that california is the highest state in this chart, it once again prove that this state is econmically strong. The difference is big between california and the rest, but most states are doing good. Texas is also the second state with average income per person, and New York is the third. We can see that the GDP numberws matches with the average income per person in each state.
